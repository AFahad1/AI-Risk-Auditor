# Instructions

To help you complete the assessment as efficiently as possible:

1. If you are asked about production or operational environments that are not applicable, please respond with "Out of Scope" so the auditor understands that the control does not apply to your organisation.
2. Where questions ask whether a procedure exists, you may state that appropriate policies and procedures are in place where applicable.
3. Wherever possible, reference the technologies you use to support the relevant control. For example: Microsoft Intune, OneDrive for data transfer, Microsoft 365, AWS, or other tools within your technology stack.
4. If you are unsure about a question, feel free to ask the auditor for clarification before responding.

You will be taken through **32 questions** covering a range of compliance and security controls. The assessment should take approximately **35–45 minutes** to complete.
